The definition

Coat designates the color(s) of a horse. The names of the dresses as well as their definitions are linked to their visual appearance, defined by the color of the hair and mane that compose it. These colors range from black to white through all the ranges of “brown”. Since the middle of the 20th century, constant progress in genetics has made it possible to explain scientifically and with increasing precision the reasons for the coloring of the horse's coat. It is now possible to statistically predict the coat of an unborn foal based on the genetic sequencing of the parents. The results of this research made it possible to establish classifications of horse coats that are closer to genetic reality.
